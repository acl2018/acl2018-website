Abstract#==#We present a semantic parser for Abstract Meaning Representations
  which learns to parse strings into tree representations of the
  compositional structure of an AMR graph. This allows us to use
  standard neural techniques for supertagging and dependency tree
  parsing, constrained by a linguistically principled type system. We
  present two approximative decoding algorithms, which achieve
  state-of-the-art accuracy and outperform strong baselines.
