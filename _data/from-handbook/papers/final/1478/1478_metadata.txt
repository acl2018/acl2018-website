Abstract#==#In this paper, we propose a new strategy, called Text Deconvolution Saliency
(TDS), to visualize linguistic information detected by a CNN for text
classification. We extend Deconvolution Networks to text in order to present a
new perspective on text analysis to the linguistic community. We empirically
demonstrated the efficiency of our Text Deconvolution Saliency on corpora from
three different languages: English, French, and Latin. For every tested
dataset, our Text Deconvolution Saliency automatically encodes complex
linguistic patterns based on co-occurrences and possibly on grammatical and
syntax analysis.
