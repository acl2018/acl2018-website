Registration is now available online, and the deadline for early bird registration closed on 4th June, 2018. Further details are available below. If you encounter any technical problems with the registration process, please email the conference secretariat. All prices are in Australian Dollars (AUD). On-site registration will also be available in the level 2 foyer on all days of the conference from 7:30am, Sunday 15th July, but as noted below, the fees are higher.

ACL Membership Requirement

Conference registrants must be members of the Association for Computational Linguistics to register for the ACL 2018 conference. You should check your membership status before registering for the conference; you will have the opportunity to renew during registration if you do not have an active membership. Membership rates are:

  • $150 for standard memberships
  • $75 for students

Membership entitles you to electronic notification of new issues of the CL journal, discounts on publications for participating publishers, access to ACL conference and workshop registration where membership is required, announcements of ACL and related conferences, workshops, and journal calls of interest to the community, membership in the society devoted to promoting CL research throughout the world, and numerous other benefits described at the ACL website.

Main Conference Registration

Full registration includes

  • coffee breaks
  • complimentary recruitment event (limited to first 400 people)
  • admission to all technical / thematic sessions
  • Student Research Workshop
  • three poster/demo/lunch sessions
  • exhibits
  • social event at Melbourne Aquarium on Tuesday 17th of July
  • welcome reception on Sunday 15th of July
